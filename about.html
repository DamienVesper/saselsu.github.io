ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

SASE is dedicated to the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their full career potential. In addition to professional development, SASE also encourages members to contribute to the enhancement of the communities in which they live.

SASE’s mission is to:

  • Prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world.
  • Celebrate diversity on campuses and in the workplace.
  • Provide opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities.

SASE membership is open to people of all ethnic backgrounds.

OUR HISTORY

SASE LSU was founded in February 2019 with just 25 active members. Unfortunately, SASE LSU failed to gain members due to COVID-19. It created a gap between the LSU community and its students. The stagnancy, virtual meetings, and developments of AAPI violence around the country caused students in the LSU community to isolate. During this time, SASE LSU focused on bringing in available faculty, designing activities, and showcasing current students and faculty to help bring awareness and discussions that would revolve around empowerment and community. Going into Fall 2021, SASE LSU gained momentum through the executive board’s focus on building community. With this new objective and slow decline of constraints, we were able bring students together after a long period of isolation. We did a boba social, miniature painting and picnic event, giveback nights at local Asian businesses, and consistent volunteering events with Cat Haven and Habitat for Humanity to help foster a safe space and also a visible presence with LSU and Baton Rouge. The continuous persistence over the semester spurred the growth from 25 to 133 active members by Spring 2022. We continued to focus on visibility and community by celebrating our diversity such as through a Lunar New Year meeting. With the growth, we began the incorporation of workshops and guest speakers so our members can also have professional advancement. SASE LSU is a space where members can celebrate their diversity despite the odds, foster their own community within LSU, and above all to love purple and live gold.

THE THREE PILLARS

Professional Development

Did you know that SASE members have received co-op opportunities through SASE? Being a SASE member has many benefits that can line you up for your next job! In addition, SASE hosts workshops to develop leadership and professional skills. You can learn interview skills, have your resume reviewed, establish technical skills, and much more.

Community Service

Do good and feel good while helping those in need. Making contributions to our local community is an important aspect of SASE. Being a good leader involves helping those around you, and we uphold this philosophy by volunteering and participating in community service events around the city.

Celebrating Diversity

Explore different cultures throughout SASE. Whether it be on campus or in the workplace, we encourage an environment where we can learn from people from all different backgrounds.
