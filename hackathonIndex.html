Love Purple, Live Gold About Our Hackathon

Geaux Hack is back for Fall 2023! After the immense success of our previous event in the fall of 2022, we couldn't wait to bring the hackathon back for another thrilling edition. This time, it's going to be even bigger and better! We will be giving you a total of 40 hours to unleash your creativity and turn your ideas into reality.

At Geaux Hack, we're dedicated to empowering you to take your skills to new heights. Whether you come solo or with a team, we're here to support you every step of the way. Throughout the event, we'll provide you with all the necessary resources and a team of mentors who are eager to help you work on something cool or learn new skills.

From the moment you step in, you'll be welcomed into a vibrant community of like-minded individuals, from newcomers to seasoned veterans. It's a perfect opportunity to network, collaborate, and share knowledge with other creators and professionals. Don't miss this amazing weekend of innovation, camaraderie, and fun. Geaux Hack Fall 2023 promises to be an experience you'll cherish forever. So, join us, and let's create something extraordinary together!

One Team, One Heartbeat Frequently Asked Questions

General

A hackathon is weekend long creative marathon where college students team up to brainstorm and build awesome projects. It's all about hustling, coding, and presenting your creations to impress judges and fellow hackers!

Projects generally fall under one (or more) of the six categories: Web Development, Android, iOS, AI/ML, AR/VR, Game Development.

Registration

Just fill out the registration form at the top of the page and you'll be all good!
